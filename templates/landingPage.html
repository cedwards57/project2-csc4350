Functionalities

Our app not only gives users the ability to search for and discover new recipes, but it also provides a space for users to create and consolidate their grocery lists.

Why Does It Matter

Most people's gorcery lists are either random pieces of paper, notes on cell phones, or notebooks with scribles and changes everywhere. This app puts all of the ingredients a person needs into one place and does all the math to go along with it.

Who Can Use It

Everyone! This app was created for the person who just wants to try new things or the unorganized home chef. This app helps users stay organized, even if grocery lists are just a fraction of users lives.
